## Annual leave

Official employees receive 12 paid annual leave days per calendar year. For every 5 continuous years of service at the company, an employee receives 1 additional annual leave day. Probation time is not counted immediately for annual leave, but after signing the official contract the probation period is counted toward seniority for annual leave.

## Paid personal leave

Employees receive 3 paid days for their own marriage. Employees receive 1 paid day when their child gets married. Employees receive 3 paid days when a parent, parent-in-law, spouse, or child passes away. Other family bereavement cases follow labor law, usually 1 day.

## Leave registration

For leave shorter than 3 days, employees must notify at least 2 working days in advance. For leave of 3 days or more, employees must register at least 1 week, or 7 days, before the planned leave date. Leave is registered through the online HR portal and must be approved by the direct manager.

## IT and security

To reset or change the company email password, employees must submit an IT support ticket or contact the IT department directly. Company laptops must use strong passwords. Employees must not leave laptops unattended in public places and must return equipment in good condition when leaving the company. Requests for peripherals such as mouse, keyboard, or monitor must be created in the asset management system and confirmed by email to Administration IT.

## Working hours and benefits

The company works from 08:00 to 17:00, Monday through Friday. Lunch break is from 12:00 to 13:00. Work on holidays and Tet is paid at least 300 percent of the normal daily wage. Lunch allowance is defined in each employee's labor contract. Employees can register for internal training through the training portal or propose training to their manager during quarterly review.

## Insurance and health

The company pays compulsory social insurance, health insurance, and unemployment insurance according to law. Employees with at least 1 year of seniority receive a voluntary premium health insurance package. Female employees receive 6 months maternity leave and social insurance benefits. For long sick leave, employees must submit medical confirmation to HR within 3 days after returning to work.

## General rules

Employees should wear professional clothing at the office. Employees with indefinite-term contracts must provide at least 45 days notice before resignation.
