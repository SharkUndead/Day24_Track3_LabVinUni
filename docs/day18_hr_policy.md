## Annual leave

Official employees receive 12 working days of annual leave each year. Employees with seniority receive 1 extra annual leave day for every 5 continuous years of work.

## Public holidays

Employees receive paid days off for Vietnamese labor-law holidays, including 1 day for New Year, 5 days for Lunar New Year, 1 day for Reunification Day, 1 day for International Labor Day, 2 days for National Day, and 1 day for Hung Kings Commemoration Day.

## Paid personal leave

Employees receive paid personal leave in the following cases: 3 days for their own marriage, 1 day for a child's marriage, and 3 days when a parent, parent-in-law, spouse, or child passes away.

## Leave registration process

Employees must register annual leave through the internal system at least 2 days before the leave date for annual leave shorter than 3 days. For leave of 3 days or more, employees must register at least 1 week in advance.
